# Privacy Policy for Chicken Radar Native

**Data Processing:** 
Chicken Radar Native does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or screen content to external servers. All image processing (using OpenCV) is performed locally on the user's device.

**Permissions:**
- **Screen Capture:** Used solely for real-time pattern analysis within the application.
- **Overlay:** Used to display radar graphics on top of other applications.
